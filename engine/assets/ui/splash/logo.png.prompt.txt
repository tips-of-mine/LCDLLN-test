SUBJECT
A cinematic dark fantasy game splash logo featuring a massive Black Moon
as the central emblem of the universe, symbol of ancient corruption and forbidden power.
The logo must feel monumental, legendary, and ominous.

COMPOSITION & UI CONSTRAINTS
Centered composition designed specifically for a splash screen.
Clear readable silhouette even when scaled down.
The Black Moon symbol dominates the logo.
If typography is present, it must be secondary and integrated cleanly.
Background must be transparent or extremely dark and simple, so the logo can be overlaid easily.

MATERIALS & DETAILS
The Black Moon appears carved from obsidian stone or blackened celestial rock,
with ancient fractures and subtle arcane engravings.
Edges slightly worn, ancient erosion, sacred/forbidden feel.
Optional: a thin ritual halo (very subtle) made of worn metal or stone, engraved with faint runes.
Typography (if included): gothic medieval, carved or forged, aged, slightly damaged, not modern.

LIGHTING & MOOD
High-contrast cinematic lighting.
Cold blue rim light outlining the emblem for readability.
Very subtle inner purple glow leaking from cracks (not neon, not bright).
Deep shadows, oppressive mystical atmosphere, “end of an age” vibe.

ART STYLE
Epic dark fantasy, gothic medieval, baroque influence.
Realistic fantasy illustration / high-end concept art.
Serious, grim tone, no humor, no cute elements.

TECHNICAL QUALITY
Ultra high resolution, razor-sharp edges, clean logo finish.
Designed for professional game UI usage, crisp silhouette, no messy artifacts.

NEGATIVE PROMPT
cartoon, anime, manga, chibi, stylized cute,
sci-fi, cyberpunk, futuristic symbols,
neon colors, overly saturated bright colors,
modern typography, flat childish icon style,
blurry, noisy, grainy, low resolution,
text artifacts, watermark, random letters, misspelled words
